OPD Appointment


Please Note :

1. No additional amount is charged other than the respective consultant fees.

2. Rescheduling an appointment - You can reschedule an appointment only once within the next 48 hrs beyond which the amount will be forfeited.

3. VACCINATION - Please book an appointment for vaccination also so that you don't have to wait in the OPD area when you visit the hospital. The amount deposited online will be adjusted in the vaccination bill generated when you visit the hospital.

4. FOLLOW UP VISITS - Follow up visits in the first 48 hrs will be complimentary. Follow up visits between 48 hrs-7 days will be charged 70% of the respective consultant's fees. But you are still required to book an appointment online to avoid any overcrowding/waiting in the OPD area. The excess amount deposited online will be refunded to you when you visit the hospital.

5. Patients visiting the hospital during routine OPD hours without an online appointment will not be entertained.

6. In case of any problem encountered while booking online, please call at 011-46056464.
